## Features

- **Founder Equity Setup** - Configure founding team and initial equity splits
- **SAFE Notes** - Post-money SAFEs with valuation caps, discounts, MFN, and pro-rata rights
- **Convertible Notes** - Debt financing with interest rates, terms, valuation caps, and discounts
- **Priced Rounds** - Series A, B, C+ equity rounds with pre-money valuations and option pool sizing
- **Employee Options** - Reserve and grant equity to key personnel
- **Exit Simulation** - Model acquisition scenarios and see your payout after liquidation preferences
- **Cap Table Visualization** - Real-time ownership percentages at each funding stage
- **CSV Export** - Download your simulation data as a spreadsheet
- **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes
- **Mobile Responsive** - Works on all devices
